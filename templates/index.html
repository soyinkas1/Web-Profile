My Brief Biography

I was born in the ancient city of Ibadan, Nigeria. I had my initial education in Ibadan up until my first university degree. I relocated to Lagos, Nigeria due to my acceptance into the highly competitive Management Trainee Programme of defunct Gateway Plc. After spending a good number of years in banking business analysis and development, my growth mindset and engineering background kicked in and I expanded my career to Project Management and qualified as a PMI certified project manager in 2017. Having being exposed to the use of computers just after leaving secondary school, I kept this passion alive learning and mastering various computer applications to varied levels of proficiencies. Currently based in London, UK after formalising my data science and data analysis skills with an M.SC degree, I continue to help solve real-life business challenges in a continuous learning environment
